About Sri Lankan Cuisine

Sri Lankan cuisine is a vibrant and diverse blend of spices, herbs, and fresh ingredients, influenced by various cultures over centuries. It features rice and curry as the staple meal, accompanied by a variety of vegetable, fish, and meat dishes. Coconut, curry leaves, pandan leaves, and an array of spices create rich, aromatic flavors that are unique to Sri Lanka. Street food like Kottu Roti and Hoppers are popular favorites enjoyed across the country.
